Nohkalikai Falls is the tallest plunge waterfall in India, located near Cherrapunji in Meghalaya, one of the wettest places on Earth. The waterfall drops from a staggering height of 1,115 feet (340 meters), creating a stunning visual as it crashes into a pool of vivid turquoise below. Surrounded by mist-covered cliffs and lush rainforests, it offers breathtaking views, especially during and after the monsoon season.
