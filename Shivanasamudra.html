Located in Karnataka, India, Shivanasamudra Falls is a majestic segmented waterfall on the Cauvery River. It splits into two main falls—Gaganachukki and Bharachukki—creating a breathtaking view, especially during the monsoon. The area also hosts one of Asia’s first hydroelectric power stations, built in 1902.
