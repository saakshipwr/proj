Dudhsagar Falls, meaning “Sea of Milk”, is a majestic four-tiered waterfall located on the Mandovi River, straddling the Goa and Karnataka border. It cascades from a height of approximately 1,017 feet (310 meters) and is especially spectacular during the monsoon, when it's in full force.
