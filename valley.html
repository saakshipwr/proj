Valley of Flowers National Park is a stunning UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Uttarakhand, India. It's famous for its vibrant meadows filled with rare and exotic flowers, set against a backdrop of snow-capped mountains. The park is also home to animals like snow leopards, red foxes, and Himalayan black bears. It blooms beautifully between July and September, making it a dreamy spot for trekkers and nature lovers.
